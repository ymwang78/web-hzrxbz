    Pharmaceutical packaging equipment needs further efforts towards automation
    Time:2020-08-20

    With the development of the medical field, various countries pay more and more attention to the research and development of pharmaceutical packaging machinery. There is still a big gap between my country's pharmaceutical packaging machinery and the equipment in developed countries as a whole, so domestic equipment must work hard in many aspects to meet the needs of the development of the pharmaceutical market.

    Pharmaceutical packaging machinery plays a key role in the quality, shelf life, sales and circulation, use and cost of drugs. At home and abroad, especially in Europe and the United States, the research and development of pharmaceutical packaging machinery are paying more and more attention. After rapid development in recent years, my country's pharmaceutical packaging machinery has experienced a process from relying on imports to relying on imitating to gradually improving itself. It can be said that it has reached a small peak on the road of industry development.

    However, there is still a considerable gap between China’s pharmaceutical packaging machinery as a whole and the equipment in developed countries, such as poor innovation ability, unstable operation of the whole machine, and low degree of integrated production, which are far from the rapid development of the pharmaceutical industry. There is still a certain gap in industrial production.

    Pharmaceutical equipment is a special profession that integrates pharmaceutical technology, chemical machinery, refrigeration, automatic control, packaging machinery, manufacturing technology, welding, automatic control, and computer application. The idea of ​​pharmaceutical equipment R&D is to integrate these related majors into the entire design confirmation process. However, there are very few people currently engaged in pharmaceutical equipment R&D who can skillfully take care of two or three of them, and it is difficult for a single professional talent to develop ideas. It's suitable to inject these professional elements into it.

    At present, although the number of professional disciplines of pharmaceutical equipment in colleges and universities is increasing, the professional teaching level is not satisfactory, there is no basic teaching material, and the professional theoretical and practical application level of the teaching staff is not skilled enough. Therefore, in the research and development of new products, we must innovate ideas, one is to train multi-professional talents, and the other is to closely cooperate with manufacturers.

    The new version of GMP has stricter requirements for pharmaceutical packaging equipment. In order to meet the GMP production process requirements, my country’s pharmaceutical packaging equipment needs to be greatly improved. The advancement of pharmaceutical packaging equipment to automation requires further efforts. The industry must continuously optimize scientific and technological resources, improve innovation efficiency, and at the same time continue to carry out standards improvement work to promote the healthy and long-term development of the industry.
