    Home-made pharmaceutical packaging machinery should shift prices to technology
    Time:2020-08-20

    Due to technical shortcomings in China, most of the pharmaceutical packaging machinery in China is mainly engaged in price wars to attract foreign businessmen. With the increasing requirements of demanders, low prices are no longer the focus of their pursuit. Under the background of fierce competition, domestic production Pharmaceutical packaging machinery should shift the focus of price to technology, and upgrading technology is the kingly way.

    According to the survey and statistics of the China Machinery Industry Federation, China’s packaging machinery industry still maintains an annual growth of about 16%. This is because the packaging industry, as a supporting industry in many areas of the national economy, is an industry that promotes consumption and demonstrates value. Technological advancements and supporting services can have a profound impact on other industries.

    my country's packaging machinery products are facing fierce international competition due to the lack of variety, low technical level, and poor product reliability. On the previous development road, domestic packaging machinery is indeed very popular due to its low price. However, with the improvement of industry requirements, pharmaceutical companies have gradually diversified their requirements for packaging machinery, and price can no longer become a competitive advantage.

    Faced with this situation, domestic medicine packaging machinery needs to further accelerate the replacement of packaging machinery. Intelligence will enter the entire packaging machinery and production lines. Therefore, the industry urgently needs to strengthen the reliability, safety, and unmanned operation of equipment and production lines. Introduce modern high-precision skills, electronic skills, microelectronics skills, marginal skills, and vague skills to strengthen competition.

    New-type packaging machinery is often a mechatronics device that fully utilizes the results of information products. The designers of some Chinese companies are not familiar with electronic products, and they dare not or will not choose them, which greatly limits the improvement of packaging machinery. For example, many new foreign packaging machinery mostly adopt separate transmission, which greatly shortens the transmission chain of the whole machine, greatly simplifies the structure, and greatly improves the working accuracy and speed. One of the core technologies here is the adoption of the synchronization technology of multi-motor drive. In fact, it is not difficult to master this technology, but some designers do not understand this development trend of packaging machinery. Another core technology is the large-scale adoption of servo drives, but old designers would not design and select servo mechanisms and their control systems.

    Advanced packaging machinery adopts a large number of new detection systems to ensure the stable and reliable operation of packaging machinery. With the increasing penetration of automation manufacturers into the industry and the development of information technology, more and more designers have accepted advanced automation technology and used advanced automation products to improve equipment performance.
