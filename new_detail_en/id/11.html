    my country's pharmaceutical packaging machinery will truly embark on the international stage
    Time:2020-08-20

    Generally speaking, packaging makes the appearance of goods more beautiful, and makes consumers want to consume. Especially in the pharmaceutical industry, the quality and safety of medicines cannot be ignored. Pharmaceutical packaging machinery will develop with the trend. In the future, Chinese medicines Packaging machinery will really set foot on the international stage.

    China’s internationalization is the goal pursued by pharmaceutical packaging machinery in the future. It is not enough to shout slogans. If pharmaceutical packaging machinery wants to truly set foot on the international stage and move towards a higher end, it must conform to the development trend of the times and transform the modern Technology is integrated into mechanical production to complete the efficient and intelligent equipment.

    Products produced in my country to meet the needs of the international market require international-level packaging. In the field of medicine, the demand for packaging has also increased rapidly, which at a certain level has promoted the development of pharmaceutical packaging machinery.

    Looking at the entire packaging machinery industry, we found that the overall technical level of domestic packaging machinery is insufficient, unable to keep up with social needs, and unable to produce advanced equipment. In addition, there is no comprehensive development plan in the industry, and the government lacks corresponding macro-control, resulting in the relatively backward development of domestic packaging machinery.

    New-type packaging machinery is often a mechatronics device that fully utilizes the results of information products. The designers of some Chinese companies are not familiar with electronic products, and they dare not or will not choose them, which greatly limits the improvement of packaging machinery. For example, many new foreign packaging machinery mostly adopt separate transmission, which greatly shortens the transmission chain of the whole machine, greatly simplifies the structure, and greatly improves the working accuracy and speed.

    One of the core technologies here is the adoption of the synchronization technology of multi-motor drive. In fact, it is not difficult to master this technology, but some designers do not understand this development trend of packaging machinery. Another core technology is the large-scale adoption of servo drives, but old designers would not design and select servo mechanisms and their control systems.

    Advanced packaging machinery adopts a large number of new detection systems to ensure the stable and reliable operation of packaging machinery. With the increasing penetration of automation manufacturers into the industry and the development of information technology, more and more designers have accepted advanced automation technology and used advanced automation products to improve equipment performance.

    The overall level of packaging machinery in my country has been behind the level of developed countries for many years. In addition to the improvement of domestic manufacturers' technical level, the localized production of foreign manufacturers also greatly promotes the improvement of my country's packaging machinery automation level.

    The future development direction of the pharmaceutical packaging industry is to continue to apply and promote high and new technologies, to arm and strengthen the overall level of the industry, and to continue to improve and optimize technology to complete the localization of high-end packaging equipment.
